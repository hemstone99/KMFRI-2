Improved Tilapia Feed Formulation for Enhanced Growth

Patent Number: KE/P/2023/1234
Filing Date: 15 March 2023
Inventors: Dr. Jane Muthoni, Dr. Samuel Owiti
Status: Granted
Patent Office: KIPI
Expiry Date: 15 March 2043

Abstract

This invention relates to a novel feed formulation for Nile tilapia (Oreochromis niloticus) that enhances growth rates by 25-30% compared to conventional feeds. The formulation incorporates locally available ingredients including algae meal and insect protein, reducing feed costs while improving feed conversion ratios. The patented formulation also includes a probiotic mix that improves gut health and reduces disease incidence.

Biodegradable Fishing Net with Bycatch Reduction

Patent Number: KE/P/2021/9101
Filing Date: 5 June 2021
Inventors: Dr. Michael Juma, Dr. Sarah Achieng
Status: Granted
Patent Office: KIPI
Expiry Date: 5 June 2031

Abstract

A fishing net constructed from biodegradable polymer fibers that degrades after 18-24 months in marine environments, reducing ghost fishing impacts. The net incorporates selective mesh sizes and visual markers that reduce bycatch of juvenile fish and non-target species by up to 40%. The material maintains strength comparable to conventional nylon nets during use but breaks down completely in marine environments without toxic residues.
