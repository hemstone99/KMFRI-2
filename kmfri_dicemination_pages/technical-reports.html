Status of Coral Reefs in Marine Protected Areas Along the Kenyan Coast (2023)

Authors: J. Kamau, M. Achieng, P. Onyango | Report No: KMFRI-TR-2023-45 | Pages: 78 | Published: August 2023

This report presents findings from a comprehensive survey of coral reef health in Kenya's marine protected areas, including Watamu, Kisite-Mpunguti, and Malindi Marine Parks. The study documents coral cover, species diversity, and threats to reef ecosystems, providing recommendations for improved management.

Annual Fisheries Status Report for Lake Victoria (2022)

Authors: S. Mwangi, L. Nyongesa, K. Otieno | Report No: KMFRI-ASR-2022-12 | Pages: 112 | Published: March 2023

The annual status report provides an overview of fish landings, species composition, and fishing effort in the Kenyan portion of Lake Victoria. It includes data from all major landing beaches and analysis of trends in Nile perch, tilapia, and dagaa fisheries, with comparisons to previous years.

Technical Guidelines for Sustainable Seaweed Farming in Kenya

Authors: P. Muthoka, J. Wambua, et al. | Report No: KMFRI-TG-2023-08 | Pages: 64 | Published: June 2023

This technical guide provides best practices for seaweed farming along the Kenyan coast, covering site selection, farming methods, pest and disease management, and post-harvest handling. The guidelines are based on five years of research and farmer participatory trials.

Authors: KMFRI Research Team | Report No: KCDP-BS-2021-01 | Pages: 145 | Published: January 2022

Comprehensive baseline data for the Kenya Coastal Development Project, documenting ecological and socio-economic conditions in the project area prior to implementation. Includes marine resource assessments, household surveys, and institutional capacity evaluations across five coastal counties.
