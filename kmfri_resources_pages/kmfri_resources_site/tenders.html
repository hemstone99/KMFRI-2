Tender Submission Guidelines

  • All bids must be submitted in sealed envelopes marked with the tender number
  • Bids must be received by the specified closing date and time
  • Late bids will not be accepted under any circumstances
  • Bidders must provide all required documents as specified in the tender notice
  • Electronic submissions should be sent to procurement@kmfri.go.ke with the tender number as the subject
  • For inquiries, contact the Procurement Office at +254 20 1234567
