ISO & Quality Management System

Our commitment to quality standards and continuous improvement

Quality Management at KMFRI

The Kenya Marine and Fisheries Research Institute (KMFRI) is committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality in all our research and operations. Our Quality Management System (QMS) is certified to international standards, ensuring consistent quality in our services and continuous improvement in our processes.

Our Quality Policy

"KMFRI is committed to providing quality marine and fisheries research services that meet customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements. We achieve this through continuous improvement of our Quality Management System and the competence of our staff, ensuring the sustainable utilization of aquatic resources."

Our Certifications

KMFRI maintains certification to the following international standards

ISO 9001:2015
Quality Management
ISO/IEC 17025:2017
Laboratory Competence
OHSAS 18001:2007
Occupational Health & Safety
