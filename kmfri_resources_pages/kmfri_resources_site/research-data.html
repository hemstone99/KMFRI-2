Research Data Management Plan

Ensuring quality, integrity, and accessibility of marine research data

KMFRI Research Data Management Framework

The Kenya Marine and Fisheries Research Institute (KMFRI) is committed to implementing best practices in research data management throughout the entire data lifecycle. This Research Data Management Plan (RDMP) outlines our policies, procedures, and infrastructure for managing research data generated by KMFRI scientists and collaborators.

Our Data Management Vision

To be a leader in marine and freshwater data stewardship by ensuring research data are well-managed, preserved, and accessible to support scientific discovery, policy development, and sustainable resource management.

This plan aligns with national and international standards including the Kenya Open Data Initiative, FAIR Data Principles (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable), and the UNESCO IOC Oceanographic Data Exchange Policy.

Research Data Lifecycle

Our approach to managing data through all stages of research

The KMFRI data lifecycle encompasses planning, collection, processing, analysis, preservation, sharing, and reuse of research data. Each stage has specific management requirements and quality control measures.
