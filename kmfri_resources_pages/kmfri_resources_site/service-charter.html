Service Charter

Our commitment to excellent service delivery for all stakeholders

Welcome to Our Service Charter

The Kenya Marine and Fisheries Research Institute (KMFRI) is committed to providing quality services to all our stakeholders. This Service Charter outlines our commitment to service excellence, the standards you can expect from us, and how you can provide feedback on our services.

Our Service Pledge

"We pledge to provide professional, efficient, and courteous services to all our clients while upholding the highest standards of integrity and accountability in marine and fisheries research."

This charter reflects our commitment under Kenya's Constitution, the Public Service Values and Principles (Article 232), and the government's service delivery reforms. It applies to all KMFRI services including research, laboratory analyses, technical advisory, training, and public awareness programs.

Client Responsibilities

To help us serve you better, we request our clients to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information
  • Treat our staff with courtesy and respect
  • Observe our institutional rules and regulations
  • Provide feedback to help us improve
  • Pay for services promptly where applicable
  • Respect intellectual property rights
  • Use provided information responsibly
  • Keep scheduled appointments or notify us in advance
