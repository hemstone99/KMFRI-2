KMFRI Management Framework

The Kenya Marine and Fisheries Research Institute (KMFRI) Management Plan provides the strategic framework for achieving our mandate of conducting marine and fisheries research. This plan outlines our governance structure, management systems, and operational processes to ensure effective delivery of our services.

Management Vision

"To be a world-class marine research institution through excellent governance, innovative management practices, and efficient resource utilization for sustainable development of Kenya's blue economy."

This management plan aligns with Kenya's Constitution, Vision 2030, the Marine Fisheries Research Policy, and international best practices in institutional management of research organizations.

Organizational Structure

Our governance and management hierarchy

KMFRI operates under a clearly defined organizational structure that separates governance from management while ensuring effective coordination between research, administration, and support functions.

The structure comprises the Board of Management at the governance level, the Director General's office for strategic leadership, and four technical directorates overseeing research programs, supported by administrative departments.
