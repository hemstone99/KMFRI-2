Strategic Plan 2023-2027

Charting the course for marine and fisheries research excellence

Our Strategic Direction

The Kenya Marine and Fisheries Research Institute (KMFRI) presents its Strategic Plan for the period 2023-2027, outlining our commitment to advancing marine and freshwater research, sustainable fisheries management, and aquatic ecosystem conservation. This plan aligns with Kenya's Vision 2030 and the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly SDG 14 - Life Below Water.

Our Mission

To generate knowledge and innovations for sustainable utilization and management of aquatic resources through research, capacity building and partnerships.

Strategic Pillars

Our strategic plan is built on five key pillars that will guide our research and operations over the next five years:

1Research Excellence

Enhance scientific research in marine and freshwater ecosystems to generate knowledge and innovations for sustainable development.

2Policy Influence

Provide evidence-based policy advice to support decision-making in marine and fisheries management.

3Capacity Building

Develop human capital and institutional capacity for marine and fisheries research and management.

4Stakeholder Engagement

Strengthen partnerships with communities, government, academia and private sector for collaborative research and development.

5Organizational Sustainability

Enhance institutional governance, financial sustainability and operational efficiency.

Key Objectives

  • Conduct cutting-edge research on marine and freshwater ecosystems
  • Develop innovative technologies for sustainable fisheries and aquaculture
  • Enhance climate change adaptation and mitigation strategies for aquatic systems
  • Strengthen marine spatial planning and ecosystem-based management
  • Promote blue economy initiatives for coastal communities
  • Expand research infrastructure and technological capabilities
  • Increase visibility and impact of KMFRI's research outputs

Implementation Framework

The successful implementation of this Strategic Plan will be achieved through:

Governance

Strong leadership and oversight by KMFRI's Board of Management

Partnerships

Collaboration with national and international research institutions

Monitoring

Regular performance assessments and adaptive management
