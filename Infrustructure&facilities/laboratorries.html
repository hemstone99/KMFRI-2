Research Laboratories

State-of-the-art facilities for marine and freshwater research

Central Research Laboratories

Located at KMFRI Headquarters in Mtwapa, our central laboratories provide comprehensive analytical capabilities for marine and freshwater research.

Core Facilities

Water Chemistry Lab

Nutrient analysis, salinity measurements, dissolved oxygen

Microbiology Lab

Bacterial cultivation, water quality testing, pathogen analysis

Molecular Biology Lab

DNA extraction, PCR, genetic analysis of marine organisms

Fisheries Lab

Species identification, age determination, stock assessment

Field Station Laboratories

Our network of field laboratories located along the coast and inland water bodies provides localized research capabilities.

Key Field Labs

Mombasa Marine Lab

Coral reef studies, coastal ecology, mangrove research

Kisumu Freshwater Lab

Lake Victoria fisheries, water quality monitoring

Malindi Marine Lab

Marine biodiversity, turtle conservation, seagrass studies

Shimoni Marine Lab

Marine protected area research, fisheries ecology

Specialized Research Units

Laboratory Access

KMFRI laboratories are available for use by qualified researchers through our collaborative research programs. Laboratory access requires:

  • Approved research proposal
  • Safety training certification
  • Memorandum of Understanding for institutional collaborations
  • Advance booking (minimum 2 weeks notice)
