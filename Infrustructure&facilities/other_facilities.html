KMFRI Research Infrastructure

Beyond laboratories and research vessels, KMFRI maintains a network of specialized facilities to support marine and freshwater research across Kenya.

Marine Specimen Museum

Mombasa

Repository of over 50,000 marine specimens with taxonomic reference collections and digital cataloging system.

Marine Resource Center

Mtwapa HQ

Specialized library with over 20,000 volumes, digital resources, and historical research archives.

Services: Literature search, document delivery

Coral Restoration Hatchery

Malindi

Land-based coral propagation facility with capacity for 10,000 coral fragments annually for reef restoration.

Species: 15 coral species in culture

Coastal Weather Stations

6 locations

Network of automated stations monitoring marine weather parameters and climate change indicators.

Data: Real-time feeds available to researchers

Marine Science Conference Center

Mtwapa HQ

200-seat capacity facility with audiovisual equipment for workshops, seminars, and training programs.

Features: Simultaneous translation, recording
