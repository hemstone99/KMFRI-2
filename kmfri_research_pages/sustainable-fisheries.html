Sustainable Fisheries Research

KMFRI is dedicated to promoting sustainable fisheries practices through research and innovation. Our focus includes fish stock assessments, ecosystem-based management, and the socio-economic aspects of fisheries.

Research Focus Areas

Fish Stock Assessment

Evaluating fish populations and their habitats to ensure sustainable harvesting.

Ecosystem-Based Management

Integrating ecological, social, and economic factors in fisheries management.

Socio-Economic Studies

Analyzing the impact of fisheries on local communities and economies.

Fisheries Policy

Supporting the development of policies for sustainable fisheries management.

Notable Achievements

  • Implementation of community-based fisheries management plans
  • Development of sustainable aquaculture practices
  • Collaboration with regional fisheries organizations
