Fisheries Management

KMFRI's Fisheries Management research supports the sustainable use of aquatic resources by providing science-based guidelines and policy input. It addresses issues around fish stock status, harvesting patterns, and management strategies.

Focus Areas

Stock Assessment

Estimation of fish biomass and population trends to guide sustainable quotas.

Fishing Gear & Effort

Research on gear efficiency, bycatch reduction, and regulation effectiveness.

Policy Development

Scientific input into fisheries laws, licensing, and national blue economy plans.

Community Co-management

Support for Beach Management Units (BMUs) in participatory resource governance.

Achievements

  • Country-wide fisheries frame surveys conducted every 5 years
  • Collaborative research with counties on licensing and compliance
  • Development of ecosystem-based fisheries management plans
