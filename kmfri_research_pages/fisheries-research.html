Fisheries Research

KMFRI’s Fisheries Research program spans both inland and marine environments. The focus is on improving the understanding, management, and sustainability of fisheries resources across Kenya’s lakes, rivers, and coastal waters.

Research Areas

Inland Fisheries

Studies on stock dynamics in Lake Victoria, Lake Turkana, and other freshwater bodies.

Marine Fisheries

Monitoring catch trends, fish species diversity, and exploitation levels along the coast.

Post-Harvest

Innovations in fish handling, processing, and packaging to reduce losses and add value.

Fish Ecology

Research on habitat use, spawning grounds, and migration patterns of key species.

Key Achievements

  • Annual fish stock assessments published for inland and marine zones
  • Fishery catch data collected at 50+ landing sites countrywide
  • Resource maps developed to support sustainable harvest plans
