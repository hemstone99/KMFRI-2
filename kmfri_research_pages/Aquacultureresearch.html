About Our Aquaculture Research

KMFRI's aquaculture research focuses on developing innovative, sustainable fish farming systems to enhance food security, create employment, and reduce pressure on wild fish stocks.

Pond Culture Systems

Research on optimized pond designs, water quality management, and polyculture techniques for small-scale farmers.

Cage Culture

Innovations in lake-based cage farming for tilapia and other freshwater species.

Hatchery Technology

Advanced breeding techniques for improved fish seed production.
