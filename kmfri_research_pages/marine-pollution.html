Marine Pollution Research

KMFRI’s Marine Pollution research program investigates sources, spread, and impact of pollutants in Kenya’s marine environment. The work informs mitigation strategies and supports policy enforcement in coastal zones.

Key Focus Areas

Plastic Debris

Surveys on microplastics, plastic bottle density, and shoreline debris monitoring.

Oil Spills

Tracking and mapping of oil spills and hydrocarbons in port and coastal waters.

Heavy Metals

Analysis of lead, cadmium, and mercury in sediment and biota.

Eutrophication

Studies on algal blooms caused by nutrient loading from agricultural runoff.

Research Outcomes

  • Development of pollution status reports for the Ministry of Environment
  • Guidelines for marine waste management shared with coastal counties
  • Participation in regional protocols under Nairobi Convention
