Aquaculture Research

KMFRI's Aquaculture Directorate conducts cutting-edge research into fish farming systems, hatchery development, feed formulation, and disease management to improve sustainable aquaculture practices across Kenya.

Key Focus Areas

Cage & Pond Systems

Studies on optimizing cage and pond production systems in freshwater and marine settings.

Seed & Hatchery Tech

Research on larval rearing, broodstock selection, and hatchery operations.

Feed Formulation

Development of cost-effective, locally-sourced feed blends to boost growth.

Disease Management

Diagnostics and trialing of sustainable disease control methods.

Milestones & Facilities

  • Operational hatcheries supporting small-scale farmers nationwide
  • Field trials on cage culture in Lake Victoria and coastal sites
  • Collaborations with universities and international aquaculture centres

For analytical services, visit our lab equipped for water quality, fish health, and feed testing.
