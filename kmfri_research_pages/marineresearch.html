About Our Marine Research

KMFRI conducts cutting-edge marine research along Kenya's 600km coastline and territorial waters, focusing on ecosystem health, sustainable fisheries, and blue economy development. Our work supports national policies and international commitments for ocean conservation.

Coral Reef Conservation

Our scientists are pioneering coral restoration techniques to rehabilitate degraded reefs along the Kenyan coast. Research focuses on:

  • Coral bleaching resilience
  • Nursery propagation techniques
  • Community-based reef management
  • Ecological monitoring protocols

Marine Research Programs

Marine Biodiversity

Documenting and conserving Kenya's rich marine life through species inventories, habitat mapping, and protected area research.

Fisheries Science

Stock assessments, ecosystem-based fisheries management, and sustainable harvesting practices for marine resources.

Coastal Zone Management

Integrated approaches to coastal development balancing conservation needs with human activities.

Marine Pollution

Monitoring and mitigation of marine debris, oil spills, and land-based pollution sources.
