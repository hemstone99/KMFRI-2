Coastal Processes

KMFRI’s Coastal Processes research focuses on the physical and geomorphological dynamics shaping Kenya’s shoreline. It informs coastal development, erosion control, and infrastructure resilience.

Research Themes

Wave Dynamics

Measurement and modeling of wave energy and shoreline interaction.

Sediment Transport

Tracking erosion, accretion, and longshore drift patterns across the coast.

Coastal Erosion

Identification of erosion hotspots and evaluation of natural and artificial barriers.

Shoreline Mapping

High-resolution satellite and drone-based mapping for monitoring shoreline changes.

Outcomes

  • Long-term shoreline change data supporting climate adaptation strategies
  • Hydrodynamic models developed for coastal development planning
  • Technical advisories issued for erosion mitigation and sand harvesting control
