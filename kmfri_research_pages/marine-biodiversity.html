Core Areas of Study

Coral Reef Health

Monitoring reef cover, bleaching events, and reef restoration techniques.

Seagrass Ecology

Studies on seagrass distribution, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration capacity.

Mangrove Forests

Assessment of mangrove diversity, nursery roles, and rehabilitation initiatives.

Marine Fauna

Surveys and conservation of fish species, marine mammals, turtles, and invertebrates.

Key Achievements

  • Creation of biodiversity inventories across marine protected areas
  • Coral reef resilience studies supporting national adaptation plans
  • Collaboration with global networks like IUCN and UNEP-WCMC
