Oceans & Coastal Systems Research

The Directorate of Oceans and Coastal Systems and Blue Economy Research at KMFRI supports marine ecosystem sustainability through oceanographic, ecological, and socioeconomic research. It underpins national planning in marine fisheries, spatial management, and the blue economy.

Research Themes

Oceanography

Monitoring ocean currents, salinity, temperature, and sea level to understand marine climate systems.

Hydrography

Mapping seafloor and coastal morphology using sonar, drones, and research vessels.

Marine Spatial Planning

Integrating ecosystem and economic data to guide zoning and coastal development.

Coastal Biodiversity

Surveys of coral reefs, seagrasses, and mangroves to support marine protected areas and restoration.

Milestones

  • RV Mtafiti deployed for deep sea surveys and ecosystem assessments
  • Ocean buoys and data loggers deployed across coastal stations
  • Regional collaboration with WIOMSA, IOC-UNESCO, and Nairobi Convention
