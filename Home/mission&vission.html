Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles for marine and freshwater research in Kenya

Our Vision

"A Centre of Excellence in innovative research in marine, fisheries and the Blue Economy for development"

We envision Kenya as a leader in marine and freshwater research where aquatic resources are managed sustainably to support economic growth, food security, and environmental conservation.

Our Mission

"To generate knowledge and innovations for sustainable utilization and management of aquatic resources through research, capacity building and partnerships."

We accomplish this by conducting cutting-edge research, providing evidence-based policy advice, and fostering collaborations with stakeholders across sectors.

Our Core Values

Excellence

Commitment to highest standards in scientific research and service delivery

Innovation

Continuous improvement through creative solutions and new technologies

Teamwork

Collaborative approach to achieving institutional goals

Integrity

Ethical conduct and accountability in all our operations

Partnership

Building strong relationships with stakeholders

Sustainability

Balancing resource use with conservation needs

"At KMFRI, we believe that healthy oceans and freshwater ecosystems are fundamental to Kenya's prosperity. Our research provides the scientific foundation for sustainable management of these precious resources for current and future generations."

- KMFRI Director General
