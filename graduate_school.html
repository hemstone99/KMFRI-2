  • Interdisciplinary approach
  • International collaboration
  • Dissertation defense

Research Focus Areas

Sustainable Fisheries

Developing science-based approaches to fisheries management that balance ecological sustainability with economic needs.

Coral Reef Conservation

Research on reef resilience, restoration techniques, and impacts of climate change on coral ecosystems.

Marine Biotechnology

Exploring marine organisms for novel compounds with pharmaceutical and industrial applications.

Coastal Zone Management

Integrated approaches to managing coastal resources and addressing sea-level rise challenges.

Admissions Process

Application Steps

  • Submit online application
  • Provide academic transcripts
  • Complete entrance examination
  • Attend interview (if required)
  • Receive admission decision

General Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field (for MSc)
  • Master's degree (for PhD)
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on 4.0 scale
  • Research proposal (for PhD)
  • Three letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose
