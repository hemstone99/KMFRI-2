Support Services Directorate

About the Directorate

The Support Services Directorate provides the essential administrative, financial, and technical infrastructure that enables KMFRI to deliver on its research mandate. Our team of professionals works behind the scenes to ensure smooth operations across all research centers and programs.

Our Services

Finance & Accounting

Budget management, financial reporting, and procurement services for research programs.

Human Resources

Staff recruitment, development, welfare, and performance management.

ICT Services

Technology infrastructure, systems support, and data management solutions.

Administration

Facilities management, transport, and logistical support for field research.

Procurement

Transparent acquisition of goods and services in compliance with PPDA regulations.

Internal Audit

Risk assessment and assurance services for good governance.
